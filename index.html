Developer & Media Production Specialist

Chase is a media production and development specialist with experience in software, web, and game programming, film production, news, graphic design, business, and marketing. He graduated from the University of Cincinnati in 2018 with a BFA in Electronic Media and a MS in Information Technology.

As a recent graduate, Chase hopes to improve his abilities as a developer while exercising his diverse creative skillset. In his free time, he pursues other creative endeavors such as playing in bands, working as a voice over artist, developing video games, and traveling.
